## Where coastal water damage starts

Clogged gutters are quietly responsible for some of the worst damage on the coast: water pouring over the edge into your fascia and soffits, pooling at the foundation, and feeding the rot and mold that follow. When you look up **gutter cleaning near me** in Ocean Springs, you're really protecting the whole house.

Heavy Gulf rain plus lots of tree cover means gutters here clog fast — and a clog during a storm is exactly when you don't want one.
