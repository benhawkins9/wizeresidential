## A science-based approach to mold removal

On the Mississippi Gulf Coast, mold isn't a maybe — it's what humidity, leaks, and storms leave behind. The wrong fix (a coat of paint over the stain) comes back fast. **Mold remediation near you** should start by answering one question: where is the moisture coming from?

At Wise Residential I find and fix that source first, then clean and protect the structure so the problem actually stays solved. It's the same careful, by-the-book approach I've brought to every job since framing my first house.

## My multi-step recovery process

- **Inspection & containment** — assess the spread and seal the area to prevent cross-contamination.
- **Source removal** — safely remove colonized drywall, insulation, and materials.
- **Surface & HVAC treatment** — clean every affected surface, including HVAC systems and mini-splits, with EPA-approved sanitizers.
- **Restoration** — replace materials and put the space back together.
- **Prevention** — borate treatment and moisture control to inhibit regrowth.
